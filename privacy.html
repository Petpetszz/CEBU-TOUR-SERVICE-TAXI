Privacy policy

How we handle information in connection with this website and when you reach out to book a tour or transfer. For day-to-day questions, use our Contact page.

Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

Cebu Tour Service Taxi offers private tours and transport in Cebu, Philippines. For questions about this policy or your information, email info@cebutourservicetaxi.com or use the options on our Contact page.

What this website does not do

This site is for information and to help you start a conversation with us. We do not process card payments on this website, and we do not operate customer logins or a user account database on this static site.

Information we may receive

Depending on how you contact us, we may see:

  • Booking form: When you use our booking request form, your details are assembled into a message that opens in your own WhatsApp or email app. That step happens on your device; we do not receive a separate copy through a booking API on this site.
  • Email and messaging: Name, phone number, itinerary preferences, and other details you choose to send by email, WhatsApp, Facebook / Messenger, or similar channels.
  • Calls: Caller ID or numbers you share when you phone us.
  • Technical data: Like most sites, hosting and networks may process IP addresses and basic access logs as part of delivering pages. Third-party CDNs (for fonts and front-end libraries) may set technical cookies when your browser loads assets.

How we use information

We use what you share to respond to inquiries, prepare quotes, confirm services, coordinate drivers or vehicles, and keep ordinary business records (including where needed for accounting or legitimate legal obligations). We do not sell your personal information to data brokers, and we do not use this site to run targeted advertising profiles.

Sharing

We may share limited details with people who need them to deliver your trip (for example an assigned driver or partner operator for a specific leg). Messaging and social platforms you use have their own privacy terms. We recommend reading those policies if you contact us through Meta, WhatsApp, or similar services.

Phone, WhatsApp, and Facebook

When you use those channels, content and metadata are processed under the policies of the relevant provider. We treat messages we receive as confidential business correspondence and use them only as described above.

Cookies and similar technologies

We rely on common CDNs and libraries that may set cookies or local storage as part of normal browser operation. We do not add advertising pixels or social “share” trackers on this site beyond ordinary outbound links you click.

Retention

We keep booking-related messages and correspondence only as long as needed for operations, customer service, accounting, and where the law requires retention. If you want a copy of what we hold about you, or you believe something should be corrected, contact us using the details above.

Your choices and rights

Where applicable (including under Philippine data privacy law), you may have rights to access, correct, or object to certain processing. We will respond to reasonable requests that we can verify relate to you. Some rights may be limited by law or by what is needed to perform a contract you have asked us to enter into.

Security and children

We use reasonable measures suited to a small business to protect information we control. No method of transmission over the internet is perfectly secure. This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data for marketing.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will adjust the “Last updated” line at the top of this page. Material changes may also be mentioned on our home page or contact channels where practical.

About Us

Cebu Tour Service Taxi began in 2016 when our father—a longtime Cebu taxi driver—asked his sons to launch a Facebook page so guests could book tours and transfers more easily. Today we still arrange private Cebu tours, south and north runs, and hotel and airport transfers with the same straightforward, road-tested service we started with.
