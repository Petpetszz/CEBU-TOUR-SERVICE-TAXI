  • Since 2016
  • Cebu city to south & north

Who we are

From one taxi driver’s shift to a name guests remember

Our father has spent decades earning a living as a taxi driver in Cebu—long shifts, familiar streets, and guests who needed a dependable ride. He is not particularly comfortable with computers or social media, so when he wanted a clearer way to offer day tours and longer transfers to visitors, he turned to his sons for help.

In 2016 we created the Facebook page “Cebu Tour Service Taxi” under his direction: a simple name, an honest description of what he could deliver, and a way for travellers to message us without going through a faceless agency.

What started as that modest online presence has grown into the same service philosophy—straight talk, fair arrangements, and a driver who actually knows the island—now supported on our side so enquiries and updates stay prompt.

What we do

Private transport and day plans across the island

We organise private vehicles and guided-style day plans for guests who want Cebu done properly—not rushed, not vague on inclusions.

  • Cebu city & metro tours, including mountain and heritage stops when you want ridge-line views and culture in one day
  • Southern Cebu (Oslob, Kawasan / Badian, Moalboal, coastal runs) with realistic timing for distance and traffic
  • Northern Cebu and other long-distance private runs when discussed in advance
  • Hotel transfers and Mactan–Cebu airport transfers, including agreed meet-and-greet style pick-ups

We are still built around hands-on work on the road, not a glossy corporate fleet story. That shapes how we quote, plan routes, and take punctuality and safety seriously.

Where we serve

Based in Labangon, Brgy. Tisa, Cebu City, with pick-ups across Metro Cebu and Mactan. Bohol and custom routes by arrangement.

How we communicate

WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, or email—we confirm details in plain language before you travel.

How we drive

Courteous, safety-first driving and schedules that respect how far Cebu’s geography really is between stops.
