About Us

Cebu Tour Service Taxi began in 2016 when our father—a longtime Cebu taxi driver—asked his sons to launch a Facebook page so guests could book tours and transfers more easily. Today we still arrange private Cebu tours, south and north runs, and hotel and airport transfers with the same straightforward, road-tested service we started with.
