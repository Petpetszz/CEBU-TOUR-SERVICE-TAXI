Point-to-point

Airport & hotel transfers

Private car or van with driver and fuel—same team as our day tours. Share your flight or addresses and we confirm timing and price on WhatsApp or email. No payment is taken on this website.

Mactan · Cebu City · south & north Cebu

What we offer

One-way or return transfers with a dedicated driver: Mactan–Cebu International Airport (MCIA), cruise port / ferry areas where we can legally pick up, hotels and resorts in Metro Cebu and Mactan, and longer runs (for example toward the south) when you need a pre-arranged ride instead of a full tour day.

Pricing depends on distance, time of day, vehicle size, and luggage— we quote clearly before you commit.

What is usually included

  • Private vehicle and professional driver
  • Fuel for the agreed route
  • Meet at the agreed pick-up point (airport kerb / hotel lobby when allowed)

Tolls, parking, terminal fees, or venue-specific charges may be extra—we mention them in your quote when they apply.

How to book

The fastest way is our booking form: choose Airport & hotel transfer, add date, time, passenger count, and your pick-up / drop-off details. Send the request through WhatsApp or email from the form—same flow as tours.

You can also message us on Contact with the checklist below.

What to tell us

Copy this list into your message so we can quote quickly:

  • Date & time (flight arrival / departure, or hotel checkout time)
  • Flight number & airline if airport involved (helps with delays)
  • Pick-up & drop-off (hotel name + area, or terminal if known)
  • Passengers & luggage (large bags, boxes, sports gear)
  • Child seats or mobility needs if any

Quick answers

Do you meet inside the terminal? We follow whatever meet-up rules apply on your travel date; your driver will confirm the exact spot on WhatsApp.

Can we add a short stop? Often yes—mention it when you book so we can allow time in the quote.

More questions? See our FAQ or ask us directly.

About Us

Cebu Tour Service Taxi began in 2016 when our father—a longtime Cebu taxi driver—asked his sons to launch a Facebook page so guests could book tours and transfers more easily. Today we still arrange private Cebu tours, south and north runs, and hotel and airport transfers with the same straightforward, road-tested service we started with.
